welcome!

here you can listen our upcoming, unreleased EP, and get a sense of the essence of our project. this is our first work, and it encapsules our first two years of sonic quest: who we are in frequencies, and where are we taking our music. in this lapse of time, we redefined our sound and esthetic a couple of times and made big decissions, such as moving the project from the remote island of Mallorca to the more alive city of Barcelona.

this is a 4-track, 15 minutes, concept-heavy work that will be matched with a whole-length video of the same duration. the movie will guide you visually through the sounds, and it will help you resonate with the whole concept, the humble story we are telling.

disclaimer: this is by no means the final sound. the tracks have been mastered in a provisional way. the cover is also a provisional decision.
